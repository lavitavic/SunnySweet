About Sunny Sweet ٩(^ᗜ^ )و ´-

Sunny Sweet is a brand that sells donuts as its main product. The donuts sold by Sunny Sweet have different shapes for each product and each donut also has a different taste. The donuts sold by Sunny Sweet are relatively cheap and delicious. The donuts sold also have different milk content such as powdered milk, liquid milk, and sweetened condensed milk.

Sunny Sweet was created because the milk used to make donuts was not certain and we wanted to give customers a different idea of ​​the difference between donuts made with different types of milk.
